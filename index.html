Parallel coordinates. Analyze multivariate data.

Any multivariable CSV file could in principle be visualized with our implementation of parallel coordinates. This tool is highly interactive since a selection or filtering can be performed both on the rows of the table and the parallel coordinates chart, having any of the two previously mentioned updated in real time depending on the selection performed on the other.
